# Notebook Knight — Quest Location Banner Prompts

Context: every location screen (Shop, Blacksmith, Armor Shop, Inn, Library, Church, Guild Hall,
Notice Board) shows a `.location-banner` illustration at the top — a hand-drawn scene on a piece
of ruled legal-pad paper, ~2:1 landscape (delivered art is 1774×887), full-bleed with the paper
background baked into the image itself (not composited separately in CSS). `ui_banner_church.png`
is the reference for this exact style.

This list covers the same treatment for all **34 quest areas** (`QUEST_AREAS` in the game data),
in level order. Areas are unique locations, not individual quests — if more than one quest sends
the player to the same area, they already share one banner automatically, so 34 prompts is the
full set with nothing duplicated.

Each prompt below is self-contained — paste one at a time into ChatGPT image generation.

**Known gotcha:** ChatGPT/DALL-E often drifts from an exact 2:1 ratio and sometimes adds a text
caption or logo mark in a corner even when told not to — check each result and crop/regenerate as
needed before dropping it in `01_Art/Approved/UI/Banners/`.

## Shared style block (repeated in every prompt below)

Hand-drawn pencil and ink sketch of an interior or landscape scene, loose confident linework with
light-to-medium cross-hatch shading for depth and shadow — like a detailed notebook-margin
illustration, not a finished painting. Monochrome ink linework only (black ink on the paper, no
added color). The entire scene is drawn directly on a sheet of aged cream/manila legal-pad paper
that fills the full frame edge to edge: faint horizontal blue ruled lines running the full width,
and a vertical red margin line a short distance in from the left edge. No text, no captions, no
logos, no watermarks, no border/frame around the paper — the paper edge is the image edge.
Landscape orientation, roughly 2:1 aspect ratio. Same loose, slightly wobbly, confident pencil-sketch
line weight and rendering style throughout, matching a hand-drawn RPG storybook aesthetic.
